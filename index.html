FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before working with us.

Do I need to be based in Turkey or Europe to work with you?+
Not at all. We work with clients globally. What matters is that your business involves the Turkey–Europe trade corridor — whether you're sourcing, exporting, or entering a new market.
How long does a typical engagement take?+
Market briefings are delivered in 5–7 business days. Full market entry reports take 10–14 business days. Retainer engagements begin immediately from month one.
What sectors do you cover?+
We focus on three sectors: textiles & apparel, food & agriculture, and building materials. We also cover cross-cutting topics like customs & compliance and UK market access.
How is payment handled?+
We invoice via bank transfer or Wise. For project-based work, 50% is due upfront and 50% on delivery. Retainer clients are billed monthly in advance. All prices in USD.
Can you help if I don't know which service I need?+
Absolutely. The free discovery call is specifically designed for this. We'll understand your situation and recommend the most suitable engagement — with no obligation.
Do you work with Turkish exporters as well as European buyers?+
Yes — this is a core part of our differentiation. We work equally with Turkish companies looking to enter European markets and European companies sourcing from Turkey. Both directions are our expertise.
What languages do you work in?+
English and Turkish. All reports are delivered in English as standard; Turkish versions available on request at no additional cost.
Are your reports confidential?+
Yes. All client work is strictly confidential. We do not share client information, company details, or report contents with any third party without explicit written consent.
